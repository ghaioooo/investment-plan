Strict Rules \u2014 Never Break

1
Liquid Reserve (USDT 10%) is a red line. Never touch it except for buying sharp declines or real emergencies like illness or income loss.
2
Never invest with debt. You only invest from real surplus.
3
Regular DCA regardless of market. Discipline is the secret.
4
No selling before 2029. Bitcoin at least \u2014 assets need a full cycle.
5
-60% rule for Altcoins. If drops more than 60% \u2192 seriously reconsider decision.
6
3x rule for profits. When any Altcoin reaches 3x \u2192 take out original capital.
7
Rebalance every 6 months only. Don't review portfolio weekly.
8
Store in Cold Wallet. Any amount you don't actively trade should be in your cold wallet.
